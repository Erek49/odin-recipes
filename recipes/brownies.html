Dessert

Dark-Chocolate Brownies

Perfect dessert that will leave you feeling guiltless!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup raw sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tbspn Ener-G egg replacer mixed with 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbspn non-dairy milk
  • 1 1/2 tspn vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tspn apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups whole-wheat pastry flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1/2 tspn sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (dairy-free) chocolate chips or chunks

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Combine the sugars and applesauce with an electric or handheld mixer.
  3. Beat in the Ener-G mixture, soy milk, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. Combine the dry ingredients (except chocolate chips) in a separate bowl.
  5. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients, then stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Pour the batter into a sprayed 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake for 16-20 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
